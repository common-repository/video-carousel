USE

Click edit to set the video gallery. Add, edit, or delete the items you want to display the gallery. You can see here the options you have for item:

  • Youtube videos or video flv. You can put a Youtube link video or upload a flv video.
  • Title. The title of each image/video.

__________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

You can enter the url of the videos manually or press the button "change video". Pressing the button will load the library wordpress video/images, select the video you want and click "Insert in Post" to insert the video in the carousel.

 

After the items, the ultimate has the configuration options and overall design of the ultimate. These are among others: width of the ultimate, the ultimate height, number of thumbnails, the thumbnails sizes, types of animation, colors, text, ...
